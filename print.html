Creative Brief

PRINT is a local 3D printing company that works off of 2D prototypes. They need 3D artists to help perfect more complicated projects.

The PRINT business plan anticipates exponential growth in the near future. Their current reliance on project managers is not particularly scalable to a larger market, so they would like to automate tasks that do not require artist-project manager interaction via a web app.

Scope

The scope of this project includes prototypes of the web app as well as the finished product. The pages included in this app are a profile, projects, history, and payment page for the artists to interact with.
