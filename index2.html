About

I've been a web developer with a focus on the user experience since 2016. I've learned a ton about component-driven design, accessiblity, and usability. I'm not afraid to dive in and learn something new. Whether it's testing out different ways to create reusable code, researching what makes a modal accessible (or not), or doing some competitor benchmarking, I'm always busy looking into a new question.

When I'm not working, I really love getting out into nature, preferrably on a bike. It's a great way to hit the reset button after a long day in front of a screen. A fun trivia fact about me is that I once pedaled across the whole United States. I took a long break from biking after that. But I'm back in the saddle these days!
