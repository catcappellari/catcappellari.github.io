About

I've been a web developer with a focus on the user experience since 2016. I'm not afraid to dive in and learn something new. Whether it's testing out different ways to create reusable code, researching accessible functionality, or doing competitor benchmarking, I'm always busy looking into a new question.

When I'm not working, I love getting out into nature, preferably on a bike. It's a great way to hit the reset button after a long day in front of a screen. A fun fact about me is that I once pedaled across the whole United States. I've got a lot of rides left on my bucket list!
