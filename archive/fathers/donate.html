Capacity building and Technical Assistance

This focuses on sustaining the Center and also in brining much-needed services to the other local communities launching fatherhood programs.

Advocacy and Awareness

The Center actively promotes father-friendly policies and pracices which raise awareness about barriers faced by fathers.

Jobs not Jail

Support fathers who work, attend fatherhood support group, and pay their child support instead of going to jail for failing to keep up with payments.

Responsible Fatherhood

Helps fathers surmount the barriers that stand between them and being good fathers.
