Creative Brief

The South Carolina Center for Fathers and Families is an organization that seeks to help men who are struggling to be good fathers to their children. Their website exists to help fathers find resources and connect with their program as well as to encourage donations, volunteers, and the media to help support their work.

The SCCfFF would like their website to be easier to navigate, less 'busy,' and do a better job of encouraging donations

Scope

The scope of this project includes prototypes of the web site as well as the finished product. The pages included in this app are a home, about, programs, and donations page for interested parties to interact with.
