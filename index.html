LAYR Case Study

Discovery

zVerse is a local 3D printing company that works off of 2D prototypes. While most of the design is done using technology, 3D artists are needed to help perfect more complicated prints. These artists currently work closely with project managers.

The zVerse business plan anticipates exponential growth in the near future. Their current reliance on project managers is not particularly scalable to a larger market, so they would like to automate many of those tasks.

Scope

The scope of a project includes all the deliverables, such as pages, prototypes, and the final product. The LAYR Artist Network web app helps zVerse automate interactions with artists and helps the artists easily navigate through the entire project process.

The pages included in this app are a profile, projects, history, and payment page.

One Columbia Case Study

Discovery

One Columbia for Arts & History is a non-profit organization formed to support and promote tourism in Columbia, South Carolina. Their mission is to advise, amplify and advocate for the unified arts and history community. They currently use a paper passport system so their patrons can track events that they have attended.

One Columbia is seeking a visual prototype for a digital Passport in the form of a native iPhone app that uses AR Codes and geo-tagging to allow users to check into culture events.The goal of the digital Passport is to supplement, and eventually replace, their existing printed passports since the cost of printing and distributing hard materials is prohibitive. They have the additional goal of augmenting the app with features that only a mobile device can provide.

This app created for anyone in the greater Columbia area who wishes to engage in local arts and who has access to an iPhone.

Scope

The scope of this project includes wireframes, thumbnails, mockups, and an interactive prototype in InVision.

The actions included in this app are a login area, an AR check-in area, a notes/stamp collection section, and an upcoming-events details section.
