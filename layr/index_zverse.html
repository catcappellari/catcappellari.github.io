Discovery

What is zVerse?

zVerse is an applications company focused on accelerating the creation of meaningful 3D printable content. With the LAYR 3D Content Creation Platform, zVerse is creating a suite of software applications that makes any 2D content 3D printable. Since being founded in 2012, zVerse's technology has brought meaningful, high-quality 3D printed content to brands and organizations all over the world.

As part of their "turnkey" 3D printing service, zVerse is having to manage a growing 3D artist network. Until now, zVerse has managed artist interations on a case-by-case basis. However, one-on-one management isn't scalable with new business opportunities.

zVerse is building a platform called LAYR that will allow them to automate, and therefore scale, the processes of new artist evaluation, job allocation, asset management, and artist invoicing.

Who is the target audience for the site? What information/content is the audience looking for?

The LAYR Platform will assist all artists in finding projects and payments.
