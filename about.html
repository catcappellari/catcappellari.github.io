About Cat Cappellari

About

I am committed to the pursuit of new thoughts and challenges. I believe in getting down to the essence of ideas in order to bring focus to that which is truly important. I aim to bring clarity, brevity, and beauty to my web designs.

I took The Iron Yard's User Interface Design bootcamp in Columbia, SC, this fall. I developed valuable skills in Adobe Photoshop/Illustrator, as well as learned HTML, CSS, and the beginnings of JavaScript. All of these were studied in a process that replicated the fast-paced environment of the web design field, from sketching to prototypes to the actual site build.

I am very aware that I have only begun learning all there is to learn about this field. At the moment I am reading 'Thinking with Type' (a discussion on typography) and 'JavaScript and JQuery: Interactive Front-End Web Development' to continue building my skillset.
