Prototype & Integrations

InVision is an online tool that uses clickable areas and animations to simulate real interactions using user-designed mockups.

  • InVision Demonstration

Discovery: Creative Brief & Scope

One Columbia for Arts & History is a non-profit organization formed to support and promote tourism in Columbia, South Carolina.They currently use a paper passport system so their patrons can track events that they have attended.

One Columbia is seeking a visual prototype for a digital Passport in the form of an app that uses AR Codes and geo-tagging to allow iPhone users in the Columbia area to check into culture events and adding features that only a mobile device can provide. This will also help reduce costs associated with paper passports.

The scope of this project includes wireframes, thumbnails, mockups, and an interactive prototype in InVision.

The actions included in this app are a login area, an AR check-in area, a notes/stamp collection section, and an upcoming-events details section.
